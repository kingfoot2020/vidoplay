Privacy Policy for VidoPlay

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Introduction

VidoPlay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our VidoPlay mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Stream URLs: URLs you enter to play video content
  • Favorites/Bookmarks: Streams you choose to save for quick access
  • Watch History: Records of streams you've watched (stored locally on your device)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports, performance data
  • Network Information: Network type (WiFi/cellular) for optimizing streaming quality

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: Collects advertising ID, device information, and usage data for serving personalized advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: Collects device and usage information. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects usage and performance data. Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's streaming functionality
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze app usage and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Watch history and favorites are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal data on our servers
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • No Account Required: VidoPlay does not require account creation or login

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share data with:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes (subject to their privacy policy)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View data stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Clear watch history and favorites within the app
  • Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads in your device settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the app and all associated data

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Clear watch history: Settings → Clear History
  • Clear favorites: Settings → Clear Favorites
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Children's Privacy

VidoPlay is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Content Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: VidoPlay is a media player tool. We do not host, provide, or distribute any video content. Users are solely responsible for the content they access through the app. Users must ensure they have the legal right to access any content they play.

Cookies and Tracking

VidoPlay may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze app usage
  • Serve personalized advertisements

You can control cookie preferences through your device settings.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

  • Watch History: Retained until you clear it or uninstall the app
  • Favorites: Retained until you remove them or uninstall the app
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for 26 months

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
